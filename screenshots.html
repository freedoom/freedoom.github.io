Since Freedoom is not a game engine multiple game engines are used here to showcase it. The Chocolate Doom screenshots are based on 320x200 rendering output, scaled losslessly to 1600x1200 for an authentic 1990s FPS feel with crisp pixels. The GZDoom screenshots are 1920x1080 with default pixel smoothing settings; they excel at small background details at the cost of blurry foreground details.
