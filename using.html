Since Freedoom is free, some other projects have used Freedoom's assets. We think this is a great use of the project and should be encouraged. (Please be mindful of the license, though.)

Here are an arbitrarily selected few that we know about. If you find any others, please let us know by filing an issue or pull request on the website project at GitHub.

Applications

  • Freedoom for Android - a GZDoom Android port that comes pre-bundled with the singleplayer Freedoom campaigns.

Standalone Games

  • Blasphemer - a project to do for Heretic what Freedoom does for Doom.

  • Nocturne In Yellow - a fantasy medieval first-person shooter where "you play as a dude marching through ancient castles with the realization that every myth and story ever made in the history of man is true and all of them are pissed off at you for having a pulse and four functional limbs."

  • Cyber Griffin - a Freedoom-based sidescroller.

  • Terrorized - a horror action RPG FPS.

  • Lost In Hell - a game that combines elements from Freedoom and The People’s Doom.

  • Doominator Wave Survival - a wave-based survival FPS.

  • Gloomy Dungeons - a very retro-style FPS, think like pre-Doom, pre-Rise of the Triad.

Mods

  • Barista - a ZDoom mod “inspired by the Doom Bible, Marathon and Half-Life. It uses resources derived from Freedoom and various other sources, enough to replace everything needed by the single map and therefore work as a stand-alone.”

  • Hideous Destructor - a GZDoom mod for both Doom and Freedoom that leans into the speculative fiction aspects of the monsters and weapons, with the side effect of turning the gameplay into a tactical simulation-style shooter with some parkour elements.
